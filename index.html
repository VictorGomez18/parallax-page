About Us

1898

Los Angeles Rubber and Asbestos Company was formed in Downtown LA on Spring Street - they fabricate conveyor belts made from tan hide leathers.

2009

Matt meets Dave - 4th generation owner of Los Angeles Rubber

2016

During his one of many beer drinking sessions between Matt and his neighbor Phil Stanton, Phil tells Matt he just want a pair of welding gloves that have adequate heat protection, but are actually nimble

2016 - 2021

Matt makes dozens of prototype samples, knowing little about glove making through different factories, Phil Stanton tries them out, doesnt lose a hand in the process

2021

Matt and Phil have a design they love, but meet a real leather glove expert (Paul Wood) who tells them that what they have isnt a proper glove, redesigns happen, many more samples/prototypes

2024

First production pairs are made, Matt calls every customer for feedback, same with Aprons

2026

New versions are introduced, new products are introduced, Matt still calls ever customer possible for feedback, we continue to make the best products we can
